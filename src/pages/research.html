Understanding Evidence Levels

Meta-Analysis

Combines results from multiple studies to identify patterns and draw stronger conclusions. Highest level of evidence.

Randomized Controlled Trial

Participants randomly assigned to treatment or control groups. Gold standard for establishing cause and effect.

Human Clinical Trial

Research conducted on human participants. We only include human studies—never animal or in-vitro research.

Research Library Disclaimer

The research summaries presented in this library are provided for educational and informational purposes only.

This collection includes human clinical trials only. Animal studies, in-vitro studies, review articles, and meta-analyses have been intentionally excluded to ensure that all summaries are based on original human clinical research conducted on individual ingredients.

Vitabae products are dietary supplements, not pharmaceutical drugs. Clinical studies may use different doses, standardized extracts, isolated compounds, or controlled conditions that differ from typical dietary supplement use.

The purpose of including these studies is to demonstrate that all ingredients used in Vitabae products are selected based on clinical research.
