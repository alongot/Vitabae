Our Approach

What we mean by science-based

Every formula begins with published human research, not broad claims. We look closely at who was studied, how many participants were involved, what dose was used, and for how long.

We rigorously evaluate study design, dosage range, duration, and measured endpoints before an ingredient is ever considered.

When science is still developing, we say so. If human research isn't there, neither is the ingredient.

That is not a limitation. It is how science-based supplementation is meant to work.

Dosing Philosophy

Conservative by design

A woman's body changes throughout adulthood - from metabolism and digestion to hormonal balance, stress response, and nutrient needs.

Just because an ingredient is natural does not mean it is safe at any amount. We build conservative safety margins into every formula.

How are supplement doses determined?

Doses are established for long-term daily use and remain well within recognized safety thresholds. We prioritize amounts supported by published research over doses designed to appear impressive on a label.

More is not inherently better. What matters is appropriate dosing, supported by research and delivered consistently.
